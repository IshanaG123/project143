AI PING PONG GAME!



Instructions

1. First keepyour laptop screen stright. 2. Move yourself approximately 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop. 3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and decending manner, a red dot should appear on your right wrist. 4. Now press the play button, and again move yourself 3 to 4 feet away from the laptop. 5. The red paddle is your paddle, that will move as per the movement of your wrist.
